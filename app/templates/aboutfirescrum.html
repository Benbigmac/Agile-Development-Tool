What is FireScrum?

FireScrum is a collaboration tool where software development teams can plan, track, and manage their agile project.

You may choose to customize your workflow, collaborate, and release great software using FireScrum.

Who can use FireScrum?

FireScrum caters to software development teams, big or small.

Why use FireScrum?

The reason FireScrum was created was to help teams collaborate and coordinate their work with ease.
